About Us

     Love Stray is a small, non-profit pet rescue and adoption organization that focuses on yhe safety of stray animals that need a home. This website also allows potential fur parents to look for a new furry companion, as an alternative to purchasing pets. Providing pets and yourself with a better life. Our organization believes that every pet deserves to be safe and treated in a right way. Our volunteers offers training and orrientation for new pet owners to ensure a smooth transitioning. The goal of our organization is to give home to our furry friends who are abandoned and neglected. Our organization was established on March 16, 2022, it was created by small group volunteers.
